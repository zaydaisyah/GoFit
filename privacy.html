Data & Privacy

Last Updated: January 01, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, purchase a membership, book a class, or communicate with us. This may include your name, email address, phone number, and payment information.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services, including processing transactions, managing your account, sending technical notices, and responding to comments and questions.

3. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no data transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

4. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to personalize content, analyze our traffic, and improve your user experience. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect site functionality.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data with trusted service providers who help us operate our business, subject to confidentiality agreements.
